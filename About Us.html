About Us

Welcome to Earth Savior, a social business dedicated to protecting the environment by repurposing used cooking oil into eco-friendly soaps. Founded in April 2024 by a group of five final-year BBA students from Albukhary International University.

We aim to reduce oil waste and educate the community about its environmental impact. Our mission and vision center on mitigating pollution, preventing water and soil contamination, and protecting aquatic ecosystems and wildlife. By transforming waste oil into useful products, we aim to lessen environmental harm and safeguard water and soil quality.

Our purpose extends beyond repurposing oil. We strive to educate the community on the environmental risks of oil waste, promoting sustainable practices and greater environmental responsibility.

The impact of our efforts is significant. By preventing used cooking oil from harming water sources and soil, we protect local ecosystems and wildlife, ensuring a healthier environment for future generations.

Join us in our mission to repurpose waste and protect our planet. Together, we can make a difference, one soap at a time.
